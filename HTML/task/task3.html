App devolpment

App development is the process of creating software applications that run on devices like smartphones,tablets,or computer.There apps can be for Android,IOS,or web platforms.

It involves;

planning(what the app should do)
Designing(How it looks and feels)
coding(writing the actual program)
testing(checking for bugs and errors)
launching(publishing to play store or app store)
updating(fixing issues and adding features)

types of app development

native apps - made for one platform (eg., Android or ios)
web apps - run it browsers(like websites that feel like apps)
hybrid apps - Mix of native and web technologies

why app development is important

smart phone usage is huge and people prefer apps over websites.
apps make life easier: shopping,chatting,learning,booking-all in one click
companies need apps to reach more people and grow their business
